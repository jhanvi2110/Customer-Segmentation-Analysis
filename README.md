# Customer Segmentation Dashboard – Overview

This interactive **Customer Segmentation Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of customer purchasing behavior, revenue generation, and category-wise performance. The dashboard is designed to help businesses identify key customer trends, optimize sales strategies, and improve decision-making through data-driven insights.

## Key Insights

* **Total Revenue:** 251.51M generated across all customer segments.
* **Total Customers:** 99K customers analyzed.
* **Average Revenue per Customer:** 2.53K.
* **Product Categories:** 8 major categories included in the analysis.

## Dashboard Features

### Revenue & Customer Metrics

The top KPI cards provide a quick overview of:

* Total Revenue
* Quantity Sold
* Total Customers
* Average Revenue per Customer
* Total Categories

### Payment Method Analysis

The dashboard compares revenue contribution by different payment methods:

* Cash transactions generate the highest revenue.
* Credit and Debit cards follow as secondary payment options.

### Monthly Customer Trend

A monthly trend analysis helps identify:

* Customer fluctuations throughout the year
* Peak and low engagement periods
* Seasonal purchasing behavior

### Category-Wise Customer Distribution

The donut chart visualizes customer distribution across categories such as:

* Clothing
* Cosmetics
* Food & Beverage
* Technology
* Shoes
* Books and more

### Revenue by Category

The treemap highlights the highest revenue-generating product categories, helping businesses identify:

* Best-performing segments
* High-value product areas
* Market demand patterns

### Revenue vs Quantity Analysis

The scatter plot compares:

* Revenue generated by each category
* Quantity sold
* Overall category performance efficiency

### Shopping Mall Revenue Analysis

The bar chart displays revenue contribution from different shopping malls, enabling:

* Location-based sales analysis
* Identification of top-performing malls
* Better business expansion planning

## Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI** for dashboard development and visualization
* **Data Cleaning & Transformation** using Power Query
* **DAX Measures** for KPI calculations
* **Interactive Visualizations** for business insights

## Business Value

This dashboard helps organizations:

* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Improve product targeting and segmentation
* Monitor revenue trends effectively
* Support strategic business decisions with analytics
