# 📊 Customer Segmentation Analysis

This repository showcases **interactive dashboards built using Power BI and Microsoft Excel** to analyze customer behavior, revenue trends, and product performance.

The project helps businesses gain **data-driven insights** into customer segmentation, purchasing patterns, and sales optimization.

---

# 🚀 Power BI Dashboard

![Power BI Dashboard](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/df378c1b-6148-44fb-ac5f-b7004197eb66)

## 🔍 Overview

The **Power BI Customer Segmentation Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of customer purchasing behavior, revenue generation, and category-wise performance.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- **Total Revenue:** 251.51M  
- **Total Customers:** 99K  
- **Average Revenue per Customer:** 2.53K  
- **Product Categories:** 8  

---

## 📊 Features

### 🔹 Revenue & Customer Metrics
- KPI cards for revenue, customers, and quantity sold  
- Quick business performance overview  

### 🔹 Payment Method Analysis
- Cash is the most used payment method  
- Credit & Debit cards follow  

### 🔹 Monthly Trends
- Identifies seasonal patterns  
- Highlights peak and low engagement periods  

### 🔹 Category Distribution
- Donut chart for category-wise customer distribution  
- Covers Clothing, Cosmetics, Food & Beverage, etc.  

### 🔹 Revenue by Category
- Treemap showing top-performing categories  
- Helps identify high-value segments  

### 🔹 Revenue vs Quantity
- Scatter plot comparing efficiency of categories  

### 🔹 Shopping Mall Analysis
- Location-based revenue comparison  
- Identifies top-performing malls  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- **Power BI**
- **Power Query**
- **DAX**

---

## 💡 Business Value

- Improves customer segmentation  
- Supports strategic decision-making  
- Identifies high-performing categories  
- Tracks revenue trends effectively

## 🔍 Overview

The **Excel Customer Segmentation Dashboard** analyzes customer behavior across **gender, age groups, categories, and payment methods** using interactive visuals.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** 251.51M  
- **Total Transactions:** 99,457  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 298,712  
- **Average Revenue per Customer:** ~2.53K  

---

## 📊 Features

### 🔹 Gender Analysis
- Female customers dominate revenue and quantity  
- Contribute ~60% of total sales  

### 🔹 Age Group Analysis
- Middle-aged customers generate highest revenue  
- Adults are second-highest contributors  

### 🔹 Category Analysis
- Clothing is the top-performing category  
- Followed by Cosmetics and Food & Beverage  

### 🔹 Payment Analysis
- Cash is the most preferred method  
- Followed by Credit and Debit cards  

### 🔹 Category vs Gender
- Female customers lead across most categories  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Pivot Tables  
  - Pivot Charts  
  - Slicers  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Female customers are the most valuable segment  
- Middle-aged group drives maximum revenue  
- Clothing dominates overall sales  
- Strong link between demographics and purchasing behavior
